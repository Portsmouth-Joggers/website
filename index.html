About Us

Portsmouth Joggers is a friendly & inclusive running club for runners in Portsmouth and the surrounding area.

With almost 300 members, we have runners of all abilities, from complete beginners and social joggers, through to competitive athletes and ultra marathon runners.

Club Nights

On club nights we split into groups of different abilities and speeds, so whatever your pace we will have a group to meet your needs.

When and Where

We meet on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at 6:50pm in the atrium of 1000 Lakeside, North Harbour, PO6 3EZ. If you're driving, please park in car park B5 or B6.

Running With Us

Questions? Contact Us!

Unsure whether a running club is for you? Come along to a club night and see what you think! You are welcome to run with us for a couple of weeks for free, before we ask you to commit to becoming a member.

If you have any other questions you can contact us via email.

Portsmouth Joggers is an England Athletics affiliated club.
