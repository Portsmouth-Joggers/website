About us

Portsmouth Joggers is an England Athletics affiliated, friendly, and inclusive running club, catering to runners in Portsmouth and the surrounding areas. Within our 200+ members we have runners of all abilities, from complete beginners and social joggers, through to competitive and ultra marathon runners.

On club nights we split into groups of different abilities and speeds, so whatever your pace we will have a group to meet your needs. Unsure whether a running club is for you? Come along to a club night and see what you think! You are welcome to run with us for a couple of weeks for free, before we ask you to commit to becoming a member.

We meet on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at 6:50pm in the atrium of 1000 Lakeside, North Harbour. If you're driving, please park in car park B5 or B6.

Feeling competitive? Portsmouth Joggers enter teams in both the Hampshire Road Race League and the Southern Cross Country League each year. You don't have to be fast to take part, just an enthusiastic runner/jogger!
