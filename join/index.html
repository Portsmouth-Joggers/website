Join Us

We meet at 1000 Lakeside, North Harbour, PO6 3EZ on Tuesday and Thursday evenings.

Runners should arrive from 6.50 p.m. so that they are ready to split into groups for a 7 p.m. start.

Runners are welcome to run with the club for two weeks without making any commitment to join - after that we ask you to complete a membership form and pay.

Do you want to know more about the benefits of running at Portsmouth Joggers? Visit our benefits page.

Club Membership

Our membership year runs from 1st April to 31st March of the following year.

Cost is £12 per year (reduced pro rata if you join after April).

Click the big Join Here button to access the online membership form.

England Athletics Affiliation

If you are a keen runner and want to take part in affiliated England Athletics (EA) races, or join-in with the Hampshire Road Race League (HRRL) or Southern Cross Country League (SCCL) events then please consider signing-up for the optional EA membership too.

This costs an additional £20 (not pro rated) and gets you £2 off many EA race entry fees, and allows you to run in the SCCL races for free, as well as other additional benefits.
