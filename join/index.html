PJC Membership

Join Here

Runners are welcome to run with the club for two weeks without making any commitment to join - after that we ask you to complete a membership form and pay.

Our membership year runs from April 1st to March 31st of the following year.

Cost is £12 per year (reduced pro rata if you join after April).

Click the big Join Here button to access the online membership form.

Optional England Athletics Affiliate Membership

If you are a keen runner and want to take part in affiliated England Athletics (EA) races, or join-in with the Hampshire Road Race League (HRRL) or Southern Cross Country League (SCCL) events then please consider signing-up for the optional EA membership too.

This costs an additional £19 (not pro rated) and gets you £2 off many EA race entry fees, and allows you to run in the SCCL races for free, as well as other additional benefits.
