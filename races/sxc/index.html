Summer Cross Country

Race Directors Tony Quinn, Ally Smith and Sue Clarke invite you to enter the PJC Summer Cross Country (SXC), which takes place on a Wednesday evening in June/July each year at the beautiful Queen Elizabeth Country Park.

It's a 5-mile hilly route; not for the faint hearted, but it is incredibly fun!
