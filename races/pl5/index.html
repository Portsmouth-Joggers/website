Purbrook Ladies 5

Results Here

The Purbrook Ladies 5 is our ladies-only road race, organised by our Race Directors Rachel Thomas and Naouele McHugh. Starting out as a 6 mile race back in 1984, the race has inevitably changed over the years. It is now a 5 mile route that takes in the lovely lanes and countryside around Purbrook, and has a reputation for some fantastic post-race goodies (if we do say so ourselves!). The race is a firm favourite amongst the female running community and sells out very quickly every year.

Transferring your place

How to transfer your entry to someone else.

  1. Login to your Racesignup Account
  2. Login to Racesignup (by tapping Manage Entry or Enter Now).
  3. Scroll down to the "Your Entries" panel where you will see an orange Transfer button against your name. Tap that Transfer button.
  4. Provide the email address of the person who is taking your place.
  5. They will get an email with instructions on how to take up their place. You will get a copy of that email. Please note that you are responsible for any transaction between you and the person taking your place.

Taking up a transferred entry.

  1. When someone transfers their entry to you, you will receive an email with a transfer code.
  2. From this page, login to Racesignup (by tapping Manage Entry or Enter Now).
  3. If you don't yet have a Racesignup account you will be prompted to create one.
  4. Above the Sign Up panel you will see a green button: "I have a transfer code". Tap that button and enter your code.
  5. If your code is validated, tap on your name in the Sign Up panel and complete the entry form.
  6. You will get an email confirming that your entry has been made.
