Great South Run Training Course

The Great South Run is the highlight of the Portsmouth running calendar. 10 miles around the streets of Portsmouth and Southsea, taking in the Spinnaker Tower and HMS Victory, with the famous seafront finish.

Each year, thousands of people take part, some chasing a personal best, some unsure if they can even complete it, and with many people using it as an opportunity to raise money for a cause close to their heart.

Whatever your personal goals are, a structured training plan is vital to ensure you perform to the best of your ability.
