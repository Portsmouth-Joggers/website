Great South Run Training Course

The Great South Run is the highlight of the Portsmouth running calendar. 10 miles around the streets of Portsmouth and Southsea, taking in the Spinnaker Tower and HMS Victory, with the famous seafront finish.

Each year, thousands of people take part, some chasing a personal best, some unsure if they can even complete it, and with many people using it as an opportunity to raise money for a cause close to their heart.

Whatever your personal goals are, a structured training plan is vital to ensure you perform to the best of your ability.

PJC has two groups dedicated to your success.

The Great South Ready group aims to take runners of any ability, and ensure that they can complete the course, with a target finish time of 1 hour 40 minutes or more.

The Pace Cadets group is for runners aiming for a finish time between 1 hour 20 mins and 1 hour 40 mins (this group is designed for runners who can already complete a 10k at their target GSR pace).

Don't worry if you're not sure which group is right for you - you can change groups at any time to best fit your pace as your training progresses.

Starting on Tuesday 14th July, each group will follow a structured 14 week programme. This programme is designed and proven to help participants achieve their goals. The programme takes in club sessions on a Tuesday and Thursday (from 7pm), with additional weekend runs as homework (these will not be led by PJC).

The programme costs £35 for non-club members and includes club membership from the completion of the programme until the end of the current membership year (1st April to 31st March). Participants will also receive a complimentary club top upon completion of the programme. Please note that the price does not include entry to the race itself, you would need to register for the race directly with the organiser. You don't need to be registered for the Great South Run to take part in the programme.

Any current club members are entitled to participate in the course free of charge, although they would not be eligible to receive the club top.

To register for the course, please apply online by clicking on the 'Apply Here' button above. For any queries, feel free to email us at GSR@pjc.org.uk
