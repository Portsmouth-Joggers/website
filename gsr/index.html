Great South Run Training Course

The Great South Run (GSR) is the highlight of the Portsmouth running calendar. 10 miles around the streets of Portsmouth and Southsea, taking in the Spinnaker Tower and HMS Victory, with the famous seafront finish.

Each year, thousands of people take part, some chasing a personal best, some unsure if they can even complete it, and with many people using it as an opportunity to raise money for a cause close to their heart.

Whatever your personal goals are, a structured training plan is vital to ensure you perform to the best of your ability.

PJC has two groups dedicated to your success:

  • The Great South Ready group aims to take runners of any ability, and ensure that they can complete the course, with a target finish time of 1 hour 40 minutes or more.

  • The Pace Cadets group is for runners aiming for a finish time between 1 hour 20 minutes and 1 hour 40 minutes. This group is designed for runners who can already complete a 10K at their target GSR pace.

Don't worry if you're not sure which group is right for you - you can change groups at any time to best fit your pace as your training progresses.

Starting on Tuesday 14th July, each group will follow a structured 14-week programme. This programme is designed and proven to help participants achieve their goals.

The programme includes club sessions on Tuesdays and Thursdays starting at 7pm, with additional weekend runs as homework (which are not led by PJC).

If you cannot make the first session, you should still be okay to join later. This is especially true for the Great South Ready group, as their programme progresses slowly at first.

The programme costs £35 for non-club members and includes:

  • Club membership from the completion of the programme until the end of the current membership year, which runs until 31st March 2027.
  • A complimentary club top upon completion of the programme.

Please note: Race entry is not included. You will need to register for the race directly with the organiser. However, you don't need to be registered for the Great South Run to take part in the programme.

Any current club members are entitled to participate in the course free of charge, although they would not be eligible to receive the complimentary club top.
