Celebrating 50 Years of Running Together

As you may already know, 2026 is an extra special year for Portsmouth Joggers: it's our 50th birthday!

The 50th Birthday t-shirts have gone down a storm! We can’t believe how great you all look in them, and we know how gutted some of you are that you missed out. So, we are giving you all one last chance to get your hands on one! On the previous order we benefitted from the discount that came with making a huge order. Unfortunately we will not be ordering such large volumes this time round, and so the t-shirts will be priced at £25 each. The order process will be the same as previously (described below), but to make sure we can get them produced as quickly as possible, you have only 2 weeks to get your orders in. The deadline for orders will be Weds 27th May. So, what are you waiting for? Get ordering 😊

How to order

Simply fill out the order form, via the link below, and send your payment via bank transfer by 27th May 2026. All the information about how to do this is included in the order form, and the PJC bank details are also included below, for reference. Please read and follow the instructions in the form carefully, and do not submit the form until payment has been made. You are welcome to order more than one t-shirt per person, and to order t-shirts for multiple people using the same form. If ordering for multiple people, give the contact information of the person who will be collecting the order on the form. There is no need to include the contact information of every person.
