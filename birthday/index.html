Celebrating 50 Years of Running Together

As you may already know, 2026 is an extra special year for Portsmouth Joggers: it's our 50th birthday!

Fifty years of running around Pompey is quite the achievement, and we intend to celebrate in style. There will be monthly events throughout the year, including a birthday party in September, starting with the Burn’s Night Park Run on January 24th.

PJC members are busy making plans for events in almost every month of 2026 as you can see in the picture below.

Tickets for PJC 50th Birthday Party - Portsmouth Rugby Club, 7pm 26th Sept 2026

Please click on this link to request tickets for the PJC birthday event. Tickets cost £10 per person. There will be music, a full buffet and a chance to look at memorabilia and a slide show of PJC throughout the years. PJC members are welcome to request 1 or 2 tickets, the 2nd ticket can be for a partner who is not a PJC member. There is a maximum of 150 tickets available.

We have made sure we are fully kitted up for the year ahead with a bespoke PJC 50th Birthday t-shirt! The technical t-shirts have been manufactured by Scimitar, in the same material as our regular club t-shirts. They look great as you can see from the pictures below.
